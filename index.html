PUBLIX SUPERMARKET

Publix is more than just a supermarket. Publix is a family that works to provide premier customer service to all of it's customers.

Publix's History

Here are some fun facts about the history of Publix!

  • Publix was founded in 1930 by George Jenkins.
  • People still call the founder Mr. George out of respect.
  • Mr. George wanted all his associates to own a part of Publix, and today it is the largest employee-owned company in the US.
  • Publix was one of the first grocery chains in the southeast to feature an in-store bakery.
  • Publix released GreenWise as a way to focus on organic and natural food, Way before "healthy living" was trendy.
